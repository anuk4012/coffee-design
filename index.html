Coffee Design Systems

We name our Design Systems Coffee

Why Coffee Design Systems? Coffee gives instant energy with adrenaline rush and refreshing experience which resonates the personality of Happening and kind of end memorable experience clients gets when they hire Happening. Coffee can be made in wide variety by using different flavored beans with various roast levels likewise Happening serve’s wide range of consumer market with whole range of services they provide. creativity, Technical and logical aspects are applied in every step to make best coffee, all three are secret ingredients in organizing successful and memorable experienced event. Various products like milk, cream, sugar etc are used with coffee to make it more delicious like Branding, UI elements, Layouts, components to create knowledge ,reusable, Universal design system.

Happening

We transform your dream events to "Realties"

Happening is an event-production company that puts together concerts, parties, shows, and more. They are focused on promoting both their events and their services as second-to-none for a wide variety of brands and clients. Their image is energetic, youthful, and fun.

5 Keywords to Describe our Design Systems

Consistency:
The UI components,Visual Components and Code should be maintained consistently across cross platforms.
Easy to Use:
Readability, Hierarchal structure maintenance for easy to understand and Read.
Universal:
The language or content should be universally understood between designers, coders and across the organization.
Scalability:
The Design systems scales with the growth of the organization.The design systems should have the opportunity to grow, flexible as the organization grows.
Universal:
Fun, Youthful, Energetic - wanted to bring the personality of organization by bringing Humor in voice/tone,Minimalistic Design with hint of bold colors, Animations,Interactions.
